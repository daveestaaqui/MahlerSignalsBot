How it works

From raw feeds to human-readable briefs.

Every stage logs its data sources and timestamps so compliance can retrace the call.

01

Ingest verifiable feeds

Polygon OHLCV for equities plus CoinGecko spot/volume endpoints for BTC, ETH, SOL, and L2 majors.

02

Score with clear gates

Models evaluate momentum vs moving averages, volume vs 30d baselines, and dominance or flow ratios.

03

Summarize with context

Each published signal receives timeframe, expected move range, suggested stop-loss, and 2–5 rationales.
