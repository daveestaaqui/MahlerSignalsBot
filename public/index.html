About ManySignals

Built for desks blending equities with crypto majors

Daily and weekly scenario-based signals for U.S. equities plus Ethereum and Solana majors.

ManySignals pairs Polygon equities data with CoinGecko on-chain context to publish scenario-based briefs that stay conservative by default.

  • Daily/weekly scenarios for mega-cap US equities with real polygon.io data.
