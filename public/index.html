How the signal engine works

Each stage is observable, rate-limited, and grounded in real upstream providers.

1 · Ingest

Polygon equities, CoinGecko spot prices, whale-alert events, and internal ledger data stream into normalized snapshots.

2 · Score

Trend, momentum, RVOL, flow, and sentiment go through tiered gating—if an input is missing the ticker is skipped.

3 · Explain

Signals are rendered with timeframe, expected move, stop suggestion, and 2–5 rationales mixing technical + fundamental context.
